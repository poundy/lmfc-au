Training at the Lismore Model Flying Club

RC Model Flying in the Northern Rivers Wings Training and Qualifications Guide

The Lismore Model Flying Club has a number of instructors able to assist you with learning to fly RC fixed wing models. Our instructors can work with you to achieve solo flight and can award the nationally recognised MAAA Wings levels of Bronze, Silver and Gold.
