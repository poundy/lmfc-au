Sunday 26th July is the date set down for our club AGM. The day will include a club General Meeting commencing at 10am covering a few updates to members from activities underway since the last General Meeting, then flowing on into the AGM for committee member reports and then election of the Office Bearers for the next year. 

There will also be a BBQ and a fun-fly competition, and the drawing of the current raffle after the AGM completes. All money raised will go towards the costs incurred when the solar and battery system recently required maintenance.
