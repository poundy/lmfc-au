Finding the Lismore Model Flying Club

Getting to our flying field Navigate

To help you navigate to our flying field, we have listed it on Google Maps. Below is an embedded map showing our location, and navigating to this should allow you to arrive at the main entrance from Wyrallah road, which we share with the Richmond River Clay Target Club, successfully.

If you need to manually enter a street address, use:
      340 Wyrallah Road
      Lismore
      New South Wales 2480

Once through the main entrance, our gate is to the left of the Richmond River Clay Target Club entrance. This is a locked gate, that all members have access to.

We welcome the public to our field on our regular flying days. All visitors will be given a short briefing about our operating practices, and requested to sign our visitor book. We also welcome existing members from other MAAA affiliated clubs - please dont feel offended if we ask for proof of active membership if you are not known personally to our club.

For visitors wishing to attend our facilities outside our regular flying days, contact one of our club executive to see what is possible.

Twists and Turns Getting to the Parking Lot

Our access road is a single lane, with a couple of sharp corners, one with a mirror to help you see better around the corner. Park in an orderly manner to help maximise the number of vehicles we can get in the area we have.

Meet and Greet The Club House

Once you arrive, make yourself known to some of the crew and they'll find the best person to assist you in your first session, including briefing you on important safety and operational aspects of our club.
