About the Lismore Model Flying Club

RC Model Flying in the Northern Rivers About Our Club

Lismore Model Flying Club Inc (ABN 33 500 824 415), was incorporated as a registered association in 1988, however it's history goes well beyond that. The Lismore area has been synonymous with model flying since the early 1930's, with many people involved being what we now recognise as significant families in the area such as the Habib family, who had several family members in the club.

In 2017, we moved to our current flying field. At that time, an article appeared in the Northern Star, now found here for archive purposes (don't forget to look at the image library).

We now have grown to services the Greater Lismore area and Northern Rivers, and today have a membership of over 50. Our flying field is immaculately maintained and presented, even with some of the significant setbacks that have been caused by the flooding events around Lismore in recent times. A story about the club's recovery since the floods, this article was written for the MAAA.

Our regular flying days are Sunday, Tuesday, and Wednesday. We typically fly from 9am to midday, weather permitting. There are often members attending on other days for field maintenance or flying at times that suit them, so if you're coming past and see aircraft in the air, drop on in.
