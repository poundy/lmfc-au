Your Privacy with Lismore Model Flying Club

Our club Members

As a member of The Club, we store only the minimum necessary information about you, for our records and those of the associations we are affiliated with. By continuing your membership, you agree to us retaining that information for those purposes. At the cessation of your membership, your data is retained in historical records as per mandated requirements.

The Club will only use this data directly in activities related to The Club. We will not provide your data to other general members of the club without your express consent, for example we will not provide your contact number or email address to a fellow member without asking to confirm that is ok, or by passing on the other member's details.

By using the communication platforms and forums that The Club leverages but does not directly manage, for example the Facebook group, you agree to the policies of those platforms and forums that the club does not manage and cannot influence.

Using our Website

By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies in your browser. We have no specific tracking of your actions in the portion of the website that we manage; we use a framework that purports to also have minimal use of cookies, but that is beyond our control.

The site or framework may change over time and use further measures to assist your use of our site, but our intent remains the same, collect no data about you for our club.
